Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Velora Jewelry.

What materials are used in Velora jewelry?

All Velora pieces are crafted from high-quality materials including sterling silver, 14k and 18k gold, and genuine gemstones. Each product page includes full material details.

How do I care for my jewelry?

  • Store your jewelry in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Remove jewelry before swimming, bathing, or exercising.
  • Clean gently with a soft, lint-free cloth.
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this to monitor your delivery at any time.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

All Velora orders are beautifully packaged in our signature jewelry box, making them perfect for gifting. Additional gift wrapping options may be available at checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Please contact us immediately at support@velorajewelry.com if you need to make changes.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. All Velora jewelry comes with a 6-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. This does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out to us at support@velorajewelry.com — we're happy to help.
