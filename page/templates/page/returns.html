Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Velora piece. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, undamaged, and returned in their original packaging with all tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customised or engraved jewelry.
  • Earrings, for hygiene reasons.
  • Items marked as final sale.

How to Return

  • Contact us at support@velorajewelry.com within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Include your order number and reason for return.
  • We will provide you with a return shipping label.
  • Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for a different size or style, subject to availability. Please contact us to arrange an exchange before sending your item back.

Refunds

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Please allow 5–7 business days for the amount to appear in your account after we process your return.

Please Note

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or incorrect.
