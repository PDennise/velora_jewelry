Shipping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about receiving your Velora order.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery within 5–7 business days.
  • Free on orders over €100.
  • A flat rate of €5.99 applies to orders under €100.

Express Shipping

  • Delivery within 2–3 business days.
  • Available for €12.99.

International Shipping

  • We ship worldwide. Delivery times vary by destination, typically 7–14 business days.
  • International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number so you can follow your delivery every step of the way.

Please Note

Velora Jewelry is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance or courier disruptions beyond our control.
